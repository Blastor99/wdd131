Soul Flyers

Author: Miguel Gil

Site Name

Soul Flyers

This name represents a website dedicated to all the people that are passionate about airplanes and all related to aviation.

Optional domain: soulflyers.com

Site Purpose

The site aims to serve as a dedicated resource and personal platform for the aviation enthusiasm, focusing specifically on flight simulation and related hardware.

Scenarios

  • Which are the newest flight simulators and hardware for flight simulators
  • What are the best flight simulators that fits the best with my computer?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Deep Navy Blue – used for headings, footers and header background.

Accent Color: Safety Orange – used for links and highlight elements.

Typography

Headings: Merriweather, serif

Body Text: Poppins, sans-serif

These fonts provide a modern and cool environment of aviation.
